Methodology

Dataset Design

  • 20 questions hand-crafted from NCCN BCC guidelines
  • 5 categories: Diagnosis, Treatment, Systemic, Follow-up, Comparison
  • 3 difficulty levels: easy (6), medium (9), hard (5)
  • Each question has expected concepts and expected citation chapters
  • Covers BCC lifecycle: diagnosis → treatment → systemic → surveillance

Pipeline Variants

  • With Verifier: Full 9-node LangGraph pipeline including VerifierAgent
  • Without Verifier: Same pipeline with verifier replaced by a passthrough node
  • Both variants share identical retrieval, JSON extraction, and citation validation layers
  • Self-consistency agent runs in both variants

Metrics Definitions

  • Citation Accuracy: valid_claims ÷ total_claims (from citation validator)
  • Unsupported Claim Rate: claims flagged by verifier ÷ total claims
  • Concept Hit Rate: expected concepts found in answer ÷ total expected
  • Regeneration Rate: questions triggering ≥1 citation retry ÷ total questions

Limitations

  • Results depend on which NCCN PDFs are ingested — chapter names must match
  • Concept hit rate is lexical (keyword matching), not semantic
  • Citation accuracy uses fuzzy string matching (threshold 0.35) — may over- or under-count
  • 20 questions is a small sample; real evaluation needs 100+ for statistical significance
  • No human annotator ground truth — metrics are system-level proxies
